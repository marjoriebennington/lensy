Marjorie Bennington

Hi! I love using natural light and outdoor settings to capture my subjects' true beauty. I want my photos to tell a story, so I work with clients to incorporate sentimental elements and little details into our photoshoot so that the final images are truly their own. Because of this, each shoot is unique, which is one of the reasons I love my job so much! I am based in Washington, D.C., but am available for travel in other states, or around the world!
